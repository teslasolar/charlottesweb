THE OVERVIEW

Charlotte has 60 seed packs to design and ship. She needs labels, inventory tracking, artwork, planning tools, and eventually automation. This toolkit maps her seed business operations onto ISA industrial standards through the bloom's 7 prime phases — starting with the lowest-hanging fruit and scaling up only when each phase stabilizes.

Rule: each phase must work before the next begins. Don't build the storefront before the labels exist. Don't automate before you can track inventory. The seed grows from the root.
